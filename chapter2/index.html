Short Answer Takeaways

  • A polynomial cannot have negative or fractional exponents.
  • A binomial has exactly two terms.
  • The degree of the sum of two polynomials of degree n is ≤ n.
  • Zero of a polynomial p(x) is a value a such that p(a)=0.
