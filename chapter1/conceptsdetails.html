Formulas Section

  • Rational number: p/q, q ≠ 0
  • Product of surds: √a × √b = √(ab)
  • Quotient of surds: √a / √b = √(a/b)
  • Conjugate rationalisation: 1/(a-b) × (a+b)/(a+b) = (a+b)/(a²-b²)
  • Laws of exponents:
    • ap × aq = ap+q
    • (ap)q = apq
    • ap × bp = (ab)p
    • ap / aq = ap-q
